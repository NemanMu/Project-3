ABOUT ME

My name is Neman Mustafazade, a Computer Science student at ADA University. I am interested in web development and artificial intelligence. I enjoy building simple and responsive websites using HTML and CSS. Currently, I am improving my skills in Python, C++, HTML, and CSS. I always try to learn new hobbies, and one of my recent interests is solving the Rubik’s Cube. My goal is to continue developing my skills and work on real-world software projects.

In my free time, I like to watch anime, play video games, and solve the Rubik's Cube. My favorite game is Minecraft because of its creativity and endless possibilities. There are no limitations; the only limit is our creativity.

My favorite part of Minecraft is working with Redstone systems, as they function like simplified electrical systems and make it possible to create basic logic gates.
